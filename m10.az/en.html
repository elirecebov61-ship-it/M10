Handbook

How to register in the m10?

Very easy. It won't take more than 2 minutes!
1)Find the m10 app in the AppStore or Google Play Market and install it on your smartphone.
2)Submit your phone number for quick registration.That's it.
You are ready to use your m10.

Where can I pay with m10?

Over 20,000 points of sale support m10 payments. You can find the full list of partners in the app. Pay with QR and get part of your spending back as bonuses.

How can I earn in m10?

Earn bonuses with m10 payments. When you pay for utilities, internet, mobile services, or make QR payments, part of what you spend is returned as bonuses.

How to create payment template?

We have already done this for you. Now your frequent payments are automatically added to the "My payments" section. You can rename and delete them with ease.

Is it true that money is being stolen from m10?

You can completely trust m10. We apply the latest security technologies to save your money 24/7. Unfortunately, some m10 users have encountered scammers. To protect your money, remember these key rules: do NOT SHARE, TELL, SEND, SHOW
• number of your bankcard
• CVV code of your bankcard
• OTP code (sent via SMS)
By following these simple steps, you can help prevent theft and keep your funds secure.

How to change the number in m10?

Unfortunately, we are not able to change the number, so far. But you can register from another number and use multiple accounts.

Is a commission applied to transfers in m10?

Transfers between wallets are not subject to commission. For transfers from a wallet to a card, a certain limit and a small commission apply. For transfers exceeding 5,000 ₼, a commission of 0.5% of the amount will be charged. For transfers exceeding 20,000 ₼, a commission of 0.3% of the amount will be charged.

What does 300 AZN limit mean in m10?

Users who have not completed FIN registration in m10 must not exceed a balance of 300 AZN within one month. Additionally, the total amount of payments made in m10 (Bakıkart, QR, and in-app payments) must not exceed 300 AZN within one month.

How can I top up my balance in m10 if I don’t have FIN registration?

You can top up your m10 balance using a bank card. For other methods, FIN registration in m10 is required. Your m10 balance must not exceed 300 AZN within one month.

What transactions can I perform in m10 without FIN registration?

Only BakıKart, QR, and in-app payments are possible. Users without FIN registration must not exceed a balance of 300 AZN within one month. Additionally, the total amount of payments in m10 must not exceed 300 AZN within one month.

How can I remove the 300 AZN transaction limit in m10?

To remove the limit, you need to complete FIN registration in m10 app.

What financial transactions cannot be performed without FIN registration in m10?

Without FIN registration, you cannot withdraw cash, transfer funds from m10 to m10 or a bank card, take a loan, or receive international transfers. To perform these transactions, FIN registration is required.

What do I need to do to earn bonuses in m10?

To earn bonuses, you must complete FIN registration in m10 app.

If I haven’t completed KYC registration in m10, can someone send money to my account?

Yes, m10 users who have completed KYC registration can send money to users who haven’t. However, please note that users without KYC are subject to a monthly transaction limit of 300 AZN.
