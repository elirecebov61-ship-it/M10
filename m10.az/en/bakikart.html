Handbook

How to buy a QR ticket?

Go to the m10 app, select the “BakıKart” section, and tap “Buy Ticket.” Select the fare based on your route. The purchase takes just a few seconds.

How to use a QR ticket?

Go to the BakıKart section on the main page, buy a ticket, and activate the QR code. Then scan the QR code in transport to complete your payment. Each QR ticket can be used for a single ride.

What if the ticket is not used?

If the ticket is not used, the amount will be refunded to your balance within 1 day.

How much does a ride cost?

With m10, the fare is set between 0.50 and 1.30 AZN, depending on the direction.

How long is the ticket valid?

The ticket will be valid for 5 minutes.
