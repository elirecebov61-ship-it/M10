Let’s answer your questions

How long does a transfer take?

On average, transfers take about 2 minutes. Processing time may vary depending on the partner and payment system. In rare cases, a transfer may take longer.

Which countries can I send money to?

Money transfers are available to over 150 countries worldwide. You can find the full list of available destinations at the top of this page.

What is the transfer fee?

m10 does not charge any transfer fees. However, the recipient’s bank may apply a fee according to its own policies.

What should I do if my transfer fails?

If a transfer fails, you will see an error message on your screen. This is usually due to transaction limits or incorrect recipient details. Please check the information provided and try again.

Are there any transfer limits?

Yes, the following limits apply:
— Daily limit: up to 2000 ₼
— Monthly limit: up to 10 000 ₼
— Total limit via other banks and apps: up to 33 000 ₼
